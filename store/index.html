Apps Won't Verify?


It is completely normal for apple to blacklist your device. Please follow the following steps if you want to un-blacklist your device.

Delete all the apps downloaded from 3rd party appstores (InstaHub, AppValley, 3Store etc) Now, do an iCloud backup of your data Disable findMyiPhone in settings Now, do this :
  *Goto Settings
  *Click General
  *Click Reset

Click Erase All Content and Settings Once device is reset, setup as normal and sign in with your iCloud account. When asked, click Restore from an iCloud Backup option and restore the backup you made earlier

Now Download any apps from InstaHub Make sure to setup NULLIFIED from the home page (Ps. Follow the tutorial for a better understanding.)

NEVER TURN OFF NULLIFIED. IF YOU DO, YOUR APPS MIGHT GET REVOKED.
