About
This app is created for all those who like to draw on their beloved devices. Decorated it is simple and clear, and the set of options includes everything you need. This includes different brushes, and if none of them fits, you can download another, or to adjust existing (size, transparency, etc.); travel and all kinds of transformation, the creation of a large number of layers with multiple modes of mixing. Separately want to note the size of the canvas, which can be up to 4K resolution.
