What is SpooferX?
SpooferX for Pokemon GO is a free iOS application that tweaks the official Pokemon GO app available in the AppStore. This free iSpoofer for POGO alternative offers similar features and options. SpooferX can be used to teleport and catch a Pokemon in a different location. SpooferX app adds additional features to Pokemon GO app..

Play Anywhere, Anytime!
Download the app on your iPhone without a computer or sideload the IPA file. Teleport to any place on the map, add routes to favorites, change the speed of moving, activate enhanced throw, enable Go Plus, Fast Catch, Stats Inventory, hide elements from the main screen like buttons, shortcuts, joystick, spoofing, etc.

Moreover, display feed of Pokemon, Raid, and Quests, activate dark mode for the interface and included map. The app offers also an option to import and export or settings with one click. Before installing the mod remember to remove the original app from your device. iOS 13 is also supported.
